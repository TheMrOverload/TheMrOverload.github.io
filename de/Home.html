About us

​Overload Studios is a game development company that was founded by a group of experienced game developers who share a passion for creating innovative and engaging video games. With a team of talented programmers, designers, and artists, we aim to create games that push the boundaries of what is possible in the world of gaming.Our goal is to create immersive and engaging experiences that players will love. 

We believe that video games should be more than just a way to pass the time, but rather a way to transport players to new worlds and experience exciting adventures.At Overload Studios, we are constantly experimenting with new game mechanics, art styles, and technology to create truly unique gaming experiences. We are dedicated to delivering high-quality games that are fun to play and provide endless hours of entertainment.We are always looking for talented individuals to join our team and help us create the next generation of video games. If you are passionate about gaming and have the skills and experience to help us create amazing games, we would love to hear from you. Contact us today to learn more about opportunities at Overload Studios.
